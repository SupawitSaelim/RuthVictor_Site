Switches for every network

Access

Access switches play a critical role in network infrastructure by offering the essential ports and functionalities to meet the connectivity needs of end-user devices. Typically equipped with multiple Ethernet ports, ranging from 8 to 48 ports, these switches facilitate connections for a variety of devices.

Learn More


Core and distribution

Core and distribution switches are the backbone of the network, providing high-speed connectivity between access switches and routing to the distribution layer. These switches are designed to handle large volumes of traffic and ensure reliable, high-performance connectivity.

Learn More


Data center and cloud

Data center and cloud switches are optimized for high-density, high-speed connectivity in data center environments. These switches are designed to support the high-performance requirements of modern data centers and cloud environments, providing the scalability, reliability, and flexibility needed to meet the demands of today's applications.

Learn More


Industrial Ethernet

Industrial Ethernet switches are designed to operate in harsh industrial environments, providing reliable, high-performance connectivity for industrial automation, manufacturing, transportation, and other industrial applications. These switches are built to withstand extreme temperatures, humidity, vibration, and electromagnetic interference, making them ideal for use in industrial settings.

Learn More


Small business and LAN compact

Small business and LAN compact switches are designed to provide reliable, high-performance connectivity for small and medium-sized businesses. These switches offer essential features and functionalities to support the networking needs of small business environments, including plug-and-play setup, easy management, and robust security features.

Learn More
