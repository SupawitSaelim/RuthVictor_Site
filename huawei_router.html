Multi-Service Control Gateway





ME60 Multiple-Service Control Gateway

The ME60 series, developed by Huawei, comprises multiple-service control gateways, functioning as a type of Broadband Remote Access Server (BRAS). These gateways serve as a unified platform for user access and management, ideally suited for industries like broadcasting, television, and education. Built on a 2T platform, the series features the industry's largest-capacity routing line card (480G) and a large-capacity NAT service line card (160G), facilitating seamless transition from IPv4 to IPv6.

Sliced packet network platform





PTN 7900 Series

Huawei introduces the industry’s pioneering Software-Defined Packet Transport Network (SPTN) solution, delivering unified multi-service transmission, high bandwidth, and differentiated quality assurance, crucial for forward-looking packet transport networks. The PTN 7900 series represents the industry's premier Software Defined Networking (SDN)-based Packet Transport Network (PTN) device, featuring substantial capacity and high bandwidth. This series offers a range of interfaces including 40 GE, 50 GE, 100 GE, and 400 GE, along with intelligent services, traffic visualization, quality measurement, and capacity predictability. It effectively supports long-term evolution and multi-service transmission for enterprises. Furthermore, it serves as a core transmission device in the production networks of industries such as electric power, transportation, and mining.
